About the hub

Where precision meets passion

Aimverse Shooting Hub is a premier, state-of-the-art sports academy dedicated to the art and science of competitive shooting, right here in Sector 86, Mohali. We cater to every level — from a first shot to national and international stage training.

FACILITY

Elite Facilities

Advanced ranges laid out across multiple levels, with dedicated setups for 10m, 25m and 50m disciplines.

TECHNOLOGY

Electronic Scoring

World-class electronic scoring targets (EST) give every athlete instant, data-driven feedback on each shot.

COACHING

Certified Instructors

Elite coaches specialising in technical regulations, match psychology and tailored athletic development.

Our core philosophy

Shooting sports go far beyond hitting a target. It's a discipline that builds unwavering focus, mental resilience, emotional control and physical endurance. Every program at Aimverse is structured to sharpen the mind under pressure, not just perfect a stance.

Disciplines

Five lanes. One standard.

From your first shot to full Olympic distance — pick the discipline that fits you.

Air Pistol

10m, single-hand precision. The standard starting discipline for new shooters.

Air Rifle

10m, supported stance. Builds breath control and trigger discipline.

.22 Pistol

Rimfire precision and timed stages, for shooters moving beyond air calibre.

.32 Pistol

Centerfire control work — recoil management and follow-through.

50m Rifle

Prone and three-position rifle at full Olympic distance.
